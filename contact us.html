About Us

Lumbini ICT Campus is one of the leading reputed and renowned instruction well known for never ending dedication and devotion to quality education and a candid temple of knowledge and wisdom. It is designed to disseminate quality education within the quality framework. It aims to help the students make the most of themselves in terms of academic and social perspectives. Lumbini ICT Campus caters bachelor level of program on; B.Sc CSIT ( Bachelor in Science. Computer Science and Information Technology) BIM ( Bachelor of Information Management ) BCA ( Bachelor of Computer Application ) BHM ( Bachelor of Hotel Management) Besides academic programmes, we offer several extra curriculum courses/packages to the students based on the needs of global market. Expertise from inside the valley and abroad the country are frequently invited to mentor the students periodically.
